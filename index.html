About Us
RAHA in Numbers

R A H A Passenger Transport is a leading Passenger Transportation & Bus rental company in UAE. We are the ideal place for quality bus rental to choose from a variety of services and a big fleet of vehicles and a big team of dedicated professionals, having a fleet of more than 50 latest A/c and Non A/C buses of 12-14-seater, 24-30-Seater, 34-Seater, 50-Seater, 66-84 Seater capacities. We provide bus rental and transportation services monthly, Quarterly and Yearly for hotels, construction, Manufacturing and contracting companies. Apart from this, we are providing services to hospitals, restaurants, event companies, and wedding parties all over the UAE. We have the facility to replace the vehicle immediately in case of any breakdown.
